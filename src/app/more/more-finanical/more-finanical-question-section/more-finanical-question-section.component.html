Transformation story

Start understanding your businesses true performance by anonymously comparing your Key Performance Indicators to that of your peers. AIM categories businesses by Standard Industry Codes and calculates the average performance of the businesses in each classification. All submitted data is anonymised and completely unattributable to any business. Each business's unique results are encrypted and can only be unlocked with their own private key. This secure process ensures an accurate comparison without any risk of disclosure to anyone
