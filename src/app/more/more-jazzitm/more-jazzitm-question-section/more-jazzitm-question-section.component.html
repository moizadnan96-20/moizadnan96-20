Multiple servers required a single monitoring window that can keep an eye on the resources of the servers and notify when the threshold is breached.

Transformation story

IBM Tivoli Monitoring (ITM) enables monitoring of servers and custom alerting provides timely notification of any resource utilization breaching the threshold. Tivoli data warehouse keeps the historic information to see the trends of utilizations.
