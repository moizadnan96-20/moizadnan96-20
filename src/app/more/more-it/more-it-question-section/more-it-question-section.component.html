Business challenge story

OpsAlert is a platform for providing Enterprise Monitoring tools to customers in the cloud. The aim of the solution is to allow customers to reduce the cost of ownership of running their event management tools, which are typically on prem and/or in cloud. Different organizations will ultimately use this one cloud solution to get integrate their collectors (probes) to get the information at one place which can then be used for better correlation, enrichment and prediction
